Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Introduction

Thrustr ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Thrustr fitness tracking application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Profile Data: Age, gender, height, weight, and fitness goals
  • Health Metrics: Body measurements for fitness calculations
  • Contact Information: Email address (if provided for support)

Health and Fitness Data

  • HealthKit Data: Steps, calories burned, weight measurements, and other health metrics (with your explicit permission)
  • Workout Data: Exercise sessions, sets, reps, weights, and training progress
  • Nutrition Data: Food intake, meal logging, and nutritional information

Location Data

We collect location data only when you explicitly enable GPS tracking for outdoor activities like running or cycling. This data is used solely to track your route and calculate distance.

Device Data

  • Motion Data: Accelerometer data for step counting during workouts
  • Bluetooth Data: Heart rate monitor connectivity and data

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide personalized fitness tracking and recommendations
  • Calculate fitness metrics like BMR, TDEE, and body fat percentage
  • Track workout progress and performance improvements
  • Sync health data with Apple HealthKit (with permission)
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support when requested

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: All your personal data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. We do not store your personal information on our servers.

Security Measures: We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

iCloud Sync: If you enable iCloud, your data may be synchronized across your Apple devices through Apple's secure iCloud service.

Third-Party Services

OpenFoodFacts: We use the OpenFoodFacts database to provide nutritional information for food items. This service may process product barcode data to return nutritional information.

Apple HealthKit: With your permission, we integrate with Apple HealthKit to read and write health data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except as described in this policy. Your health and fitness data remains private and is only used within the app for your personal tracking.

Your Rights

  • Access: View all data stored by the app within the app interface
  • Correction: Edit or update your information at any time
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data through app settings
  • Data Export: Export your data for personal use or migration
  • HealthKit Control: Manage HealthKit permissions through iOS Settings

Children's Privacy

Thrustr is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the effective date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
